Useful Apps and Online resources


Below you will find information on popular health and lifestyle apps. Most of these apps are free to download to your phone.

While apps are often a good way to track and support fitness and health the In-MINDD research team is not endorsing any particular app for use. It is not necessary to use an app to participate in the In-MINDD study. The following information is simply a guide to some of the most widely used apps which some users may find helpful.
