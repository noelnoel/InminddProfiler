Goal #5

Get Walking

  • Aim to go for a walk for 30 minutes each day or for 60 minutes 4 times a week.
  • Use a pedometer and aim to hit the 10,000 steps a day. Start slowly and build up to 10,000 steps. Week one: Aim for 5,000 steps per day. Week Two: Aim for 7,000 steps. Week Three: Aim to hit the 10,000 steps per day. You can buy a pedometer in most chemists and sports shop at a reasonable cost. (A pedometer is a device, usually portable that counts each step a person makes by detecting the motion of the persons hips)
  • Only use the car when absolutely necessary, walk to the shops and visiting friends & family
  • Take the stairs instead of lift
