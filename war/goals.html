How can I set my personalised goals?

If you click on any factors under Room for improvement, you can choose one or more of the goals listed or you can set your own goals. The goals are designed to support the lifestyle changes necessary for good brain health. If a particular goal is not working for you we encourage you to revisit the support page and choose another goal. The suggested goals are given to you as a guide to follow but you should also feel free to choose your own goals if you wish. We will also send you a weekly email to encourage you to keep working towards your chosen goals or to come back and choose different ones.

Get Walking

  • Aim to go for a walk for 30 minutes each day or for 60 minutes 4 times a week.
  • Use a pedometer and aim to hit the 10,000 steps a day. Start slowly and build up to 10,000 steps. Week one: Aim for 5,000 steps per day. Week Two: Aim for 7,000 steps. Week Three: Aim to hit the 10,000 steps per day. You can buy a pedometer in most chemists and sports shop at a reasonable cost. (A pedometer is a device, usually portable that counts each step a person makes by detecting the motion of the persons hips)
  • Only use the car when absolutely necessary, walk to the shops and visiting friends & family
  • Take the stairs instead of lift
